Oil Industry

Oil companies are key players in meeting the world's energy demands. They engage in the exploration and extraction of crude oil from underground reserves, often located in oil fields or offshore drilling sites. Once the oil is extracted, it undergoes refining processes to convert it into various useful products, such as gasoline, diesel fuel, jet fuel, lubricants, and petrochemicals.

JEKOM Oils

Oil companies invest heavily in research and development to discover new oil reserves, improve extraction techniques, and develop innovative technologies for more efficient refining and production processes. They also focus on adhering to safety and environmental standards to minimize the impact of their operations on the environment.

Service

While oil companies have played a crucial role in powering economies and fueling transportation for decades, they also face challenges. The transition to cleaner and renewable energy sources, concerns over climate change, and the pursuit of sustainable practices are reshaping the energy landscape. Many oil companies are diversifying their portfolios by investing in renewable energy projects and exploring alternative energy solutions.
